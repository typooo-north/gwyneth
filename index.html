The Caravan Press

Making Visible the Archive of Gwyneth Alban Davis

March 2017

Project Approved for funding by Heritage Lottery Fund

16th - 21st March

Initial Site Visit, Cleaning, Restoration and Cataloguing

24th June

'AnneBlume' Weekend. Talk delivered introducing project to public. We also hear from Bill Davis, Gwneth's son, on his memories of her as a printer.

10th - 13th July

Print Production - 7 edition of 60 prints produced.

1st September

Book published, a limited run of 25 editions.

2nd-3rd September

Exhibition at Florence Mine, Egremnont as part of thier 'Ground Force Extra' residency. We host a 'meet the artists' session.

31st October 2017

Project concludes with the launch of this website
